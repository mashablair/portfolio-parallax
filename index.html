ABOUT ME

I love making websites

I am a freelance Web and WordPress Developer. I love building beautiful websites and other lovely web things. When I'm not programming, I can be found playing with my adorable 1-year-old daughter Ninja, biking with my husband or dancing salsa at a local club.





Welcome to my website. I started studying programming about 2 years ago by taking some Python classes and got sucked in pretty fast. My next step was Intro to Programming classes by Udacity and a WordPress blueprint by Skillcrush. These online courses gave me a taste of Computer Science and programming in general, as well as some good tools to start creating my very own websites. But it wasn't until Udacity gave me a scholarship for their Front-End Web Development nanodegree in June 2016 that I really discovered an exciting world of the web development. Also, I am currently enrolled in a course on Visual Design learning the latest theories and technology in UX/UI.

During my studies at Udacity and Skillcrush, I've learned the latest and greatest in the Front-End Dev industry paying close attention to responsive design and site optimization. I love resurrecting unresponsive websites or building sites from scratch using HTML, CSS, JS, jQuery, Bootstrap and Grunt, but I also love the power of WordPress when customizing the themes using PHP and adding functionality.
